NV_PTR_FREE


 PURPOSE:
	Wrapper to the IDL routine ptr_free.  In conjunction with nv_ptr_new, 
	pointer allocations are tracked for debugging purposes.


 CATEGORY:
	NV/SYS


 CALLING SEQUENCE:
	nv_ptr_free, p


 ARGUMENTS:
  INPUT:
	p:	Pointer to free.

  OUTPUT: NONE


 KEYWORDS:
  INPUT: NONE

  OUTPUT: NONE


 RETURN: NONE


 STATUS:
	Complete
