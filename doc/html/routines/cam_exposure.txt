CAM_EXPOSURE


 PURPOSE:
       Returns the exposure duration of each given camera descriptor.


 CATEGORY:
	NV/LIB/CAM


 CALLING SEQUENCE:
	exposure = cam_exposure(cd)


 ARGUMENTS:
  INPUT:
	cd:	 Array (nt) of CAMERA descriptors.

  OUTPUT: NONE


 KEYWORDS:
  INPUT:  NONE

  OUTPUT: NONE


 RETURN:
       Exposure duration associated with each given camera descriptor.


 STATUS:
	Complete
