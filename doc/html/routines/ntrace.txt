NTRACE


 PURPOSE:
       Computes the traces of square matrices


 CATEGORY:
       UTIL


 CALLING SEQUENCE:
       result = ntrace(matrix)


 ARGUMENTS:
  INPUT:
       matrix:         Array (n,n,m) of nxn matrices.

  OUTPUT:
       NONE


 RETURN:
       Array (m) giving the traces of the matrices.


 STATUS:
       Completed.
