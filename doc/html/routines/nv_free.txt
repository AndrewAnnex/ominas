NV_FREE


 PURPOSE:
	Recursively frees a descriptor.  Pointers and structures are 
	dereferenced and descended, freeing any pointers encountered.


 CATEGORY:
	NV/SYS


 CALLING SEQUENCE:
	nv_free, p


 ARGUMENTS:
  INPUT:
	p:	Pointer or structure.

  OUTPUT: NONE


 KEYWORDS:
  INPUT: NONE

  OUTPUT: NONE


 RETURN: NONE


 STATUS:
	Complete
