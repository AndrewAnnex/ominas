CAM_PSF_ATTRIB


 PURPOSE:
	Computes attributes of a point-spread function.


 CATEGORY:
	NV/LIB/CAM


 CALLING SEQUENCE:
	cam_psf_attrib, cd, <attribute keywords...>


 ARGUMENTS:
  INPUT:
	cd:	 Camera descriptor.

  OUTPUT: NONE


 KEYWORDS:
  INPUT:  NONE

  OUTPUT: 
	fwhm:	Full-width at half maximum of the point-spread function.


 RETURN: NONE


 STATUS:
	Complete


 MODIFICATION HISTORY:
 	Written by:	Spitale
